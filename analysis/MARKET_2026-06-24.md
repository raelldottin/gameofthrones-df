The Sheepstealer & Vermax chest costs 120 Gold per opening and gives only a **0.625% chance per pull** of any Crimson drop. The Rhysarion market offer provides 50 guaranteed Epic relics for a dragon already proven in one of the strongest current armies.

Although the Rhysarion offer costs more per relic than the Rare Gold-market offers, its strategic value is higher because it directly strengthens the fixed Malachite–Vhagar–Rhysarion Shieldbearer formation.
