### Important correction: direct Legendary Core purchase

Legendary Cores are directly available in the Campaign market:

- **5 Legendary Cores cost 100 Campaign Favors**.
- This equals **20 Campaign Favors per Legendary Core**.
- Ten packs are available per refresh, for a maximum of **50 Legendary Cores per refresh**.

Malachite's Forest's Instinct level 2 requires 100 Legendary Cores. Buying the full requirement through the Campaign market would require:

- **20 packs**
- **2,000 Campaign Favors**
- At least **two market refreshes**

The Campaign-market offer is separate from the Relic Shards conversion market. Earlier analysis that treated conversion as the only acquisition route was incorrect.

## Relic Shards Core Conversion

The Relic Shards market also offers a conversion chain:

| Conversion | Cost | Observed availability |
|---|---:|---:|
| 1 Epic Core | 30 Rare Cores | 99 |
| 1 Legendary Core | 25 Epic Cores | 99 |

Derived conversion costs:

- **1 Legendary Core = 25 Epic Cores**
- If all Epic Cores come from conversion, **1 Legendary Core = 750 Rare Cores**
- **100 Legendary Cores = 2,500 Epic Cores = 75,000 Rare Cores**

This conversion route is far more expensive than the direct Campaign-market offer and should generally be used only to close a small final shortage.

With the observed balance of 99 Rare Cores and 10 Epic Cores, converting 90 Rare Cores would produce three additional Epic Cores, leaving 13 Epic Cores—still short of the 25 needed for one Legendary Core.

## Core Spending Guidance

Campaign Favors should not be reserved exclusively for Legendary Cores, but the direct core pack is a valid long-term purchase for Malachite.

Recommended priority:

1. Exceptional guaranteed relic offers for locked core-army dragons, especially Malachite, Vhagar, or Rhysarion.
2. Legendary Core packs when no stronger guaranteed relic offer is available.
3. Gold conversion when it enables an immediately valuable guaranteed purchase.
4. Random keys only after higher-certainty progression options.

At the observed balance of 75 Campaign Favors, save until at least 100. Do not spend 50 Gold to refresh the market, and do not convert the 99 Rare Cores yet.

## Why Guaranteed Purchases Beat the Hatchery

The Sheepstealer & Vermax chest costs 120 Gold per opening and gives only a **0.625% chance per pull** of any Crimson drop. Guaranteed relics and direct core packs provide more predictable progression.
