# Dawnseeker Army Fit — June 25, 2026

Source: Dragon Pit screenshots supplied June 25, 2026.

## Confirmed Current State

Dawnseeker is a level 41, 2★ Rare Sentinel Whelp with Spearmen affinity. The active command is Radiant Wings, and Tactical Inferno is unlocked at Habit level 1.

This corrects the earlier roster entry that listed Dawnseeker as 3★ with the ability name Dawnsong.

## Best Current Formation

- **Left:** Jagadrix
- **Vanguard:** Dawnseeker
- **Right:** Crimson
- **Troops:** Spearmen

## Why the Formation Fits

### Left flank — Jagadrix

Tactical Inferno prioritizes the left flank for **Tactical Damage Dealt**. Jagadrix occupies that preferred slot and receives +9% Tactical Damage Dealt during rounds 1 through 3 at Dawnseeker's current Habit level.

### Vanguard — Dawnseeker

Radiant Wings applies Recovery to two adjacent allies on rounds 2, 5, and 8. Placing Dawnseeker in the vanguard makes Jagadrix and Crimson adjacent, allowing both flanks to receive the Recovery effect.

Dawnseeker is also Very Fast and can temporarily increase its own Instinct and Initiative, improving both Tactical Damage and Recovery when the 30% self-buff triggers.

### Right flank — Crimson

Tactical Inferno prioritizes the right flank for **Fire Damage Dealt**. Crimson is the formation's Fire attacker and receives +9% Fire Damage Dealt during rounds 1 through 3.

Jagadrix's enemy Initiative reduction further helps Crimson because Fire Damage is mitigated by the target's Initiative.

## Star Breakpoints

- **4★ Unbroken Devotion:** increases Recovery Received by both other allies for the full battle.
- **6★ Sunbreak:** raises Radiant Wings' opening Tactical Damage Rate to at least +100% and Recovery Rate to at least +60%.
- **8★ Wind's Favor:** adds permanent Initiative to all three allies.
- **10★ First Light:** adds opening Intelligence and Instinct plus possible First-Strike for both partners.

## Investment Decision

Tactical Inferno level 2 costs 100 blue Rare cores and 100 green bat-emblem Habit cores. The observed inventory contained 24 Rare cores and 142 matching Habit cores.

Do not spend Gold to bypass the Rare-core shortage. Keep Dawnseeker in the vanguard, preserve the Jagadrix-left and Crimson-right arrangement, and upgrade Tactical Inferno once the missing Rare cores are earned normally.
