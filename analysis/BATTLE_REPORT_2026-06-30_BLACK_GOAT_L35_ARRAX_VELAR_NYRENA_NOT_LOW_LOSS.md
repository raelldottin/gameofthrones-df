## Why This Is Not Low Loss

The army had every external advantage it could reasonably ask for:

1. **Troop-type advantage** into Archers.
2. **Enemy troop-type disadvantage**.
3. **3/3 dragon affinity**.
4. **Enemy dragons had No Bonus**.
5. **Displayed difficulty was Easy**.

Even with those advantages, the army lost **4,804** troops. That is too high for a no-loss or low-loss farming label.

The problem is concentrated on the flanks:

- **Arrax lost 1,976** troops.
- **Nyrena lost 1,872** troops.
- **Velar lost 956** troops.

Velar did the center job better than the flanks did. The issue is not mainly vanguard collapse. The issue is that both flank dragons bled too much.

This report proves an important distinction:

**Full affinity is required, but it is not enough.**

A low-loss army needs full affinity, favorable matchup, and actual survival efficiency. This army had the first two and still lost too many troops.

For the current five-army low-loss plan, remove **Arrax / Velar / Nyrena** from the proven low-loss group until a better report appears.
