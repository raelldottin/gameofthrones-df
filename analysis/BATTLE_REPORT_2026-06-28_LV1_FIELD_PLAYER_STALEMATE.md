## What This Means

### 1. The farming orientation is not a PvP answer yet

**Crimson left / Dawnseeker vanguard / Jagadrix right** worked as a farming preference, but this report shows it does not currently break a player Shieldbearer tank/sustain defense.

Against Velar / Vhagar / Feskar, the Spearmen army did not lack affinity. It lacked enough PvP finishing pressure and survival against a tank-heavy enemy core.

### 2. Dawnseeker stayed as the correct vanguard, but the army still bled too much

Dawnseeker remains the correct center for the Spearmen engine. The issue is not the vanguard slot. The issue is that the army was losing the trade every round while failing to collapse the enemy side.

### 3. The flanks may be misaligned for Dawnseeker's habit

Dawnseeker's Tactical Inferno prioritizes **Tactical Damage** on the left flank and **Fire Damage** on the right flank.

The tested formation had **Crimson left** and **Jagadrix right**. That may be backwards for maximizing Dawnseeker's support. Crimson is the Fire finisher, so Crimson may need the right-flank Fire support. Jagadrix may be better on the left if the goal is to align with Tactical support and debuff pressure.

### 4. The enemy's defensive engine was stronger than it looked

The enemy had two dragons with +20% dragon stats and one dragon with no visible bonus, but still ended with **17,044 / 24,048** troops. Vhagar in vanguard plus Velar support created enough durability that the Spearmen army could not finish within 10 rounds.

## Recommendation

Do not use this formation as the default PvP anti-player setup:

**Crimson left / Dawnseeker vanguard / Jagadrix right**

For the next PvP test, keep Dawnseeker in vanguard but swap the flanks:

**Jagadrix left / Dawnseeker vanguard / Crimson right**

Reason:

1. Dawnseeker's Tactical Inferno prioritizes Tactical support on the left and Fire support on the right.
2. Crimson is the Fire finisher, so Crimson should be tested on the right.
3. Jagadrix provides debuff/control pressure and may benefit more from the left-side support shape.
4. Dawnseeker should remain center until reports prove it cannot survive.

This does not mean the swap is proven. It means the current orientation failed badly enough in PvP that the aligned-flank test is now required.

## Strategic Takeaway

The Spearmen army is still gold-standard for its own category, but this report downgrades it for PvP against player Shieldbearer tank cores.

Use it for farming and favorable matchups. Against player defenses with Vhagar/Velar-style durability, test the aligned-flank version or use the main Shieldbearer gold-standard army instead.
