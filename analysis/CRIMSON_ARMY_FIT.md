# Crimson Army Fit

## Recommended Placement

Crimson's best current fit is the Archer fire/control formation:

| Position | Dragon | Function |
|---|---|---|
| Vanguard | Syrax | Front-line protection |
| Left flank | Crimson | Fire burst and Stun |
| Right flank | Antares | Fire damage and Vulnerable |
| Troops | Archers | Positive affinity for all three |

## Why This Works

- Crimson has positive Archer affinity and receives the +20% Dragon Stats bonus when deployed with Archers.
- Bloodscale Terror attacks any lane, so Crimson can remain protected on a flank.
- Crimson deals +140% Fire Damage on rounds 2, 5, and 8.
- Antares applies Vulnerable, increasing the target's damage received and creating a strong target for Crimson's Fire strike.
- Syrax supplies the front-line body while also receiving Archer affinity.

## Current-Rarity Limitation

Crimson is currently 1★. Evaluate the formation using only:

- 20% Stun chance on odd-numbered rounds; and
- +140% Fire Damage on rounds 2, 5, and 8.

Do not count the 10★ Round 1 Stun increase or the even-round Instinct/Initiative reductions in current performance estimates.

## Alternative Formation

Crimson can also be used in a Cavalry control formation:

| Position | Dragon | Function |
|---|---|---|
| Vanguard | Seasmoke | Front-line support |
| Left flank | Crimson | Fire burst and Stun |
| Right flank | Shimmer | Tactical damage and Initiative reduction |
| Troops | Cavalry | Positive affinity for all three |

Shimmer's Initiative reduction improves Crimson's Fire Damage indirectly because Fire Damage is mitigated by the target's Initiative. This is currently a secondary option because Arulix is more developed than Crimson.

## Comparison With Tashix

- **Crimson:** stronger single-target burst, Stun control, and three known troop affinities.
- **Tashix:** stronger documented interaction with Vesper and repeated Fire attacks.

Use Crimson in the full-affinity Syrax/Crimson/Antares Archer team. Keep Vesper/Tashix/Antares available as a synergy-focused alternative for testing.

## Sheepstealer & Vermax Chest Acquisition

Crimson is present in Reward 2's Legendary pool of the Sheepstealer & Vermax Featured Dragon Chest, but is not a featured dragon.

Per chest opening:

| Crimson result | Chance |
|---|---:|
| 5 relics | 0.60000% |
| 100 relics | 0.02375% |
| 1,000 relics | 0.00125% |
| **Any Crimson drop** | **0.62500%** |

At 120 Gold per pull, an observed balance of 894 Gold permits seven openings. Across seven openings:

- Chance of at least one Crimson drop: **4.2938%**
- Chance of no Crimson drop: **95.7062%**
- Expected Crimson relics: **0.46375**

This chest should be treated as broad roster development rather than an efficient way to target Crimson. It is more useful for building multiple current five-army dragons and for pursuing the featured Vermax or Sheepstealer pity tracks.

See `mechanics/hatchery-sheepstealer-vermax.md` and `hatchery/sheepstealer-vermax.json` for the full pool and probability model.

## Validation Plan

Test Crimson and Tashix against opponents with similar troop count and dragon levels. Compare:

1. command kills;
2. total kills;
3. number of successful Stuns;
4. troop health remaining after 10 rounds; and
5. whether the battle ends in victory, defeat, or stalemate.
