## What This Means

### 1. Shadowrend vanguard worked in favorable PvE

The important new finding is that **Shadowrend can hold vanguard in a favorable Shieldbearer-vs-Archer PvE matchup** when paired with Rhysarion and Malachite.

This corrects the earlier concern that Shadowrend should not be tested center at all. Shadowrend should not be treated as a proven PvP vanguard, but this report proves the center slot can work in the right PvE matchup.

### 2. Rhysarion + Malachite covered the survival problem

The player had visible net recovery on rounds **3**, **6**, and **9**. Those are exactly the kind of turns where the Rhysarion/Malachite shell keeps the army stable long enough for Shadowrend's late engine to matter.

### 3. Shadowrend's late window likely mattered

The largest enemy-loss rounds were **Round 7** and **Round 4**, followed by **Round 9** and **Round 8**.

Shadowrend's Midnight Aura activates on rounds **7–10**, increasing Strength and Instinct for all three allies. The strong finish from round 7 onward is consistent with the army's late-fight design.

### 4. Do not overrate this as PvP proof

This fight had several favorable conditions:

- Player had troop-type advantage.
- Enemy had troop-type disadvantage.
- Enemy dragons had no affinity bonus.
- This was a PvE Black Goat encounter, not a player defense.

So this report improves the army's farming/reserve rating, but it does not make it a PvP gold-standard army.

## Recommendation

Promote this army from theoretical test to **validated PvE reserve Shieldbearer army**:

**Rhysarion left / Shadowrend vanguard / Malachite right**

Use it when:

1. you need a no-duplicate Shieldbearer backup;
2. the target is PvE;
3. Shieldbearers have advantage or at least no disadvantage;
4. you can afford a slower clear that finishes late.

Do not use it yet as:

1. a default PvP army;
2. a replacement for Rhysarion / Vhagar / Malachite;
3. a proof that Shadowrend is generally safe as vanguard.

## Strategic Takeaway

This is a good result for the five-army problem.

It shows that **Shadowrend has real value as a Shieldbearer reserve vanguard** when the rest of the shell supplies recovery and stabilization. The army is not gold-standard over the Vhagar version, but it gives a usable no-duplicate reserve direction if Vhagar is needed elsewhere.
