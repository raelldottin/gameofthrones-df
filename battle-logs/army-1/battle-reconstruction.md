## Key Mechanics Validated

1. **Vhagar (Vanguard)**: Perfect anchor. Warrior's Resilience self (-8% dmg) + Fire Ward from Bevlorin (-5% Fire). Fiery Bonds (290 Phys) strong single-target. Takes Calculated Assault debuff (-12.5% Phys) but survives. Buffs Jagadrix with Warrior's Resilience (+16% Tact) every few rounds.

2. **Jagadrix (Left)**: **Cunning Whispers = win condition**. 434 Fire (R4) + 297 Phys (R5) on Aggressive Hunter. Also debuffs enemy Inst/Init (-15.8%). Ensnare from Aggressive Hunter hurts (-19.67% Inst/Init) but Warrior's Resilience from Vhagar counters. Breath of Fire from enemy Aggressive Hunter hits Jagadrix hard (124 Fire) but survives.

3. **Bevlorin (Right)**: Nature's Reckoning applies 10% Fire DoT + 89 Phys. Consistent Basic Attack pressure (242+237+110). Fire Ward self (-5% Fire) helps vs Aggressive Hunter's Fire. Takes Blazing Onslaught (+10% Phys taken) from Aggressive Hunter but survives.

4. **Army-wide**: **Spearmen (+7% Adv) vs Cavalry (-7% Disadv)** — classic hard counter. Spearmen retain 85% HP (11,114/12,878) while Cavalry wiped. 1 Spearmen unit lost R6, 1 Cavalry unit lost R7.
