- increase Physical Damage Dealt, excluding Basic Attacks, by 1 ally in any lane, prioritizing the right flank.

The fixed flank priorities make Vaeldra suitable for a formation with a Fire attacker on the left and a Physical-command attacker on the right.

## 10★ — Siren's Call

At the start of round 1, reduce Vaeldra's Physical Damage Received for 3 rounds.

At the start of rounds 1 through 3, Vaeldra has a chance to Taunt all enemies until the end of the round. An enemy already affected by Taunt receives Stagger instead.

- **Taunt:** forces Basic Attacks against Vaeldra.
- **Stagger:** prevents Attack Modifier Commands and Basic Attacks on the target's turn.

## Development Breakpoints

- **Current 2★:** Dragon's Valor provides basic self-protection and Strength, but Vaeldra remains a reserve option.
- **4★:** Ensnare makes Vaeldra a credible offensive vanguard for mixed Physical and Fire formations.
- **6★:** Tempting Distraction directly amplifies Fire and Physical command damage after Taunt.
- **8★:** Infernal Force defines the preferred flank arrangement.
- **10★:** Siren's Call turns Vaeldra into a high-control vanguard with mass Taunt and possible Stagger.

## Crimson Formation Roadmap

Current Army 5 should remain:

- **Left:** Jagadrix
- **Vanguard:** Dawnseeker
- **Right:** Crimson
- **Troops:** Spearmen

Vaeldra should not replace Dawnseeker at 2★. Dragon's Valor alone does not provide enough team benefit to justify weakening the current formation.

At 4★, begin testing Vaeldra as a vanguard because Ensnare lowers both enemy Instinct and Initiative.

At 6★, Vaeldra becomes a stronger offensive Crimson partner because Taunt causes enemies to take increased Fire and Physical command damage.

At 8★, the planned formation is:

- **Left:** Crimson — receives Infernal Force's Fire-damage priority
- **Vanguard:** Vaeldra — supplies Lure, debuffs, and durability
- **Right:** Arrax — receives Infernal Force's Physical-command priority
- **Troops:** Spearmen, subject to maintaining 3/3 affinity

This is a future transition path, not a current five-army replacement. Vaeldra is still underdeveloped relative to Dawnseeker and Jagadrix.

## Current Investment Decision

Do not spend Gold to upgrade Dragon's Valor. Preserve green Habit cores for higher-priority upgrades, particularly Malachite's Forest's Instinct. Vaeldra's most valuable progression comes from reaching 4★, 6★, and 8★ rather than paying to move Dragon's Valor from level 1 to level 2 immediately.
