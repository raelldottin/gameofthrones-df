## Active Command — Radiant Wings

Each round:

- 30% chance to increase Dawnseeker's Instinct and Initiative by **+20%** for 1 round.

On rounds 1, 2, 4, and 7:

- deal Tactical Damage to one enemy in the same lane;
- displayed Damage Rate: **+50%**;
- Tactical Damage scales with Dawnseeker's Instinct and is mitigated by the target's Intelligence.

On rounds 2, 5, and 8:

- apply Recovery to two adjacent allies;
- displayed Recovery Rate: **+30%**;
- Recovery is enhanced by Dawnseeker's Initiative and scales with Dawnseeker's level.

At 6★ or higher, rounds 1 and 2 use a **+100% Tactical Damage Rate** and **+60% Recovery Rate** before further Habit upgrades.

## Development Breakpoints

- **Current 2★:** Tactical Inferno already provides separate left-flank Tactical and right-flank Fire amplification.
- **4★:** Unbroken Devotion improves Recovery Received for both partners.
- **6★:** Sunbreak substantially strengthens the command's opening Tactical damage and Recovery.
- **8★:** Wind's Favor adds a permanent army-wide Initiative increase.
- **10★:** First Light adds team stat amplification and possible First-Strike during the opening three rounds.

## Current Spearmen Army

Current formation:

- **Left:** Jagadrix
- **Vanguard:** Dawnseeker
- **Right:** Crimson
- **Troops:** Spearmen

The new screenshots confirm that this formation is exceptionally well aligned with Dawnseeker's kit:

- Tactical Inferno prioritizes Jagadrix on the left for Tactical Damage.
- Tactical Inferno prioritizes Crimson on the right for Fire Damage.
- Dawnseeker in the vanguard places both partners adjacent for Radiant Wings' two-target Recovery.
- Unbroken Devotion at 4★ will improve Recovery Received for those same two partners.

This is a stronger fit than treating Dawnseeker as a generic healer. The left/right positioning should remain fixed unless one of the flank dragons changes damage type.
