## Bloodscale Terror

### Current Effects

- **Odd-numbered rounds:** 20% chance to inflict **Stun** on 1 enemy in any lane for 2 rounds.
- **Rounds 2, 5, and 8:** Deal **Fire Damage** to 1 enemy in any lane at **+140% Damage Rate**.
- Fire Damage is increased by Crimson's **Intelligence** and mitigated by the target's **Initiative**.

### Stun

Stun prevents the target from:

- activating Commands;
- activating Habits; and
- launching Basic Attacks on its turn.

### Additional Effects at 10★

- **Round 1:** Stun chance increases to 40%.
- **Even-numbered rounds:** 50% chance to reduce 1 enemy's **Instinct** and **Initiative** by **-12%** for 2 rounds.
- The Instinct and Initiative reductions are enhanced by Crimson's Intelligence.

These 10★ effects are locked at Crimson's current 1★ rarity and should not be included when evaluating present-day combat performance.

## Practical Role

Crimson is a protected-flank burst/control dragon rather than a vanguard. Bloodscale Terror reaches any lane, combines strong single-target Fire Damage with Stun, and benefits from allies that reduce enemy Initiative or apply Vulnerable.
