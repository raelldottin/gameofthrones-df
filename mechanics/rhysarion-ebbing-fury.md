## Tactical Interpretation

Ebbing Fury creates a deliberate stall-and-recover timing window:

1. Rounds 1–3 suppress burst damage from both armies.
2. Durable allies are more likely to survive into round 4.
3. At the start of round 4, Rhysarion restores all three allied dragons.
4. The damage-dealt penalty expires as the formation enters its recovery phase.

The habit is especially effective in defensive or sustain-oriented formations. Higher levels increase Recovery but also suppress the allied army's early damage more heavily, which may increase the risk of ten-round stalemates in offensive battles.
