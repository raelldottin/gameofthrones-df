## Active Command — Relentless Pursuit

Visible command effects:

- Each round, has a chance to afflict one adjacent enemy with **Vulnerable**.
- On rounds 3, 6, and 9, deals Fire Damage to two adjacent enemies.
- Displayed Fire Damage Rate: **+65%**.
- Displayed Vulnerable value: **+10%**.
- At 6★ or higher, the command gains an additional **+20% Fire Damage**.

The screenshots did not expose the exact per-round chance to apply Vulnerable, so that value is intentionally left unrecorded.

## Development Breakpoints

- **Current 2★:** Blazing Onslaught already provides team-level Fire and Physical vulnerability on separate enemy lanes.
- **4★:** Dragon's Flair adds permanent personal Fire amplification.
- **6★:** Fiery Precision adds repeated anti-Slow Fire attacks and Relentless Pursuit gains its displayed 6★ Fire bonus.
- **8★:** Dragon's Intellect improves damage scaling and survivability.
- **10★:** Redemption adds permanent Intelligence and Initiative plus recurring protection from Vulnerable and Weakened.

## Upgrade Priority

Do not spend 370 Gold to raise Blazing Onslaught from level 2 to level 3. The account is missing most of both required core types, and the increase is only from +13.2% to +15.4%.

Antares's better progression path is:

1. Reach **4★** for Dragon's Flair.
2. Reach **6★** for Fiery Precision and the command's additional Fire bonus.
3. Upgrade Blazing Onslaught later using naturally earned cores.

## Syrax Archer-Army Role

Current Archer formation:

- **Left:** Tashix
- **Vanguard:** Syrax
- **Right:** Antares

Antares is correctly positioned on the right for Syrax's future right-flank Fire-damage priority. Antares and Tashix are both Fire-oriented, so the formation currently concentrates on Fire pressure rather than a balanced Tactical-and-Fire split.

Blazing Onslaught also increases Physical command damage received by the enemy right flank. That portion of the Habit is only partially exploited unless Syrax or another ally contributes meaningful non-Basic Physical damage.
