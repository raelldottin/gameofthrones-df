# Troop Matchups

Source: Army Builder screenshots supplied June 24, 2026.

Troop-type advantage and dragon affinity are separate systems:

- Troop advantage changes troop damage by **+7%**.
- Troop disadvantage changes troop damage by **-7%**.
- A matching dragon affinity provides the separate **+20% to Dragon Stats** bonus shown in battle reports.

## Observed matchup table

| Selected troop type | Advantage (+7% damage) | Disadvantage (-7% damage) |
|---|---|---|
| Shieldbearers | Archers, Siege | Cavalry |
| Cavalry | Shieldbearers, Siege | Spearmen |
| Archers | Spearmen, Siege | Shieldbearers |
| Spearmen | Cavalry, Siege | Archers |

## Practical counter cycle

For the four standard field troops shown:

- **Shieldbearers counter Archers**.
- **Archers counter Spearmen**.
- **Spearmen counter Cavalry**.
- **Cavalry counter Shieldbearers**.

All four selected field troop types were displayed as having an advantage against Siege. The supplied screenshots did not show Siege selected, so Siege's own positive matchup text is not recorded here.

## Five-army implications

- The two Shieldbearer armies should avoid Cavalry defenders.
- The Cavalry army should avoid Spearmen defenders.
- The Archer army should avoid Shieldbearer defenders.
- The Spearmen army should avoid Archer defenders.
- A full 3/3 affinity formation can still lose badly when deployed into troop disadvantage, especially against a larger army.
