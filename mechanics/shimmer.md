Shimmer is an Instinct-based support Sentinel. The command combines ally stat buffs, repeated Tactical Damage, and later Recovery.

## Active Command — Unbreakable Loyalty

Each round:

- 30% chance to select the other ally with the highest Strength;
- increase that ally’s Strength by **18%** for 2 rounds;
- increase that ally’s Initiative by **9%** for 2 rounds;
- both modifiers are enhanced by Shimmer’s Instinct.

On rounds 2, 4, 7, and 9:

- deal Tactical Damage to two enemies within adjacency;
- displayed Damage Rate: **+50%**;
- Tactical Damage scales with Shimmer’s Instinct and is mitigated by the target’s Intelligence.

At 6★ or higher, Loyal Shield adds Recovery on those same rounds:

- apply Recovery to two other allies in any lane;
- normal Recovery Rate: **+30%**, enhanced by Shimmer’s Instinct;
- when the target has Resistance, Recovery doubles to **+60%**.

## Development Breakpoints

- **Current 2★:** buffs both flanks through Crushing Force and repeatedly strengthens the highest-Strength ally.
- **4★:** Dragon’s Insight improves Shimmer’s survival and Instinct scaling.
- **6★:** Loyal Shield adds repeated two-target Recovery on rounds 2, 4, 7, and 9.
- **8★:** Unbroken Devotion increases both partners’ Recovery Received for the whole battle.
- **10★:** Sneak Attack adds Physical damage support and First-Strike for the strongest ally.

## Current Investment Decision

Shimmer is a strong long-term support project, especially at 6★. For the current 2★ version, collect Rare cores and guaranteed Shimmer relics. Do not spend Gold merely to bypass the Rare-core shortage for Crushing Force level 2.
