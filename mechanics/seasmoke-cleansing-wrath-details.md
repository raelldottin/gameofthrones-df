# Seasmoke — Cleansing Wrath Details

Source: Dragon Pit screenshots supplied June 25, 2026.

## Cleanse (Positive)

Cleansing Wrath can remove a Positive effect from an enemy. The game defines a Positive effect as an effect that increases the target's combat effectiveness.

## Infectious Wrath at 6★

When Seasmoke Cleanses an enemy, the target receives one stack of Infectious Wrath for 3 rounds.

- Recovery Received modifier: **-15% per stack** at Habit level 1
- Maximum stacks: **3**
- Nominal maximum modifier at level 1: **-45% Recovery Received**

The screenshots confirm the per-stack value and maximum stack count. They do not show whether the game applies a floor or cap to Recovery Received.

## Physical Damage at 6★

On rounds 3, 6, and 9, Infectious Wrath adds Physical Damage to two enemies within adjacency.

- Damage Rate at Habit level 1: **+30%**
- Damage Rate against Panic targets: **+60%**
- Scaling attribute: **Seasmoke's Strength**
- Mitigation attribute: **the target's Instinct**

This Physical component uses a different stat pair from Cleansing Wrath's Fire Damage, which scales with Intelligence and is mitigated by Initiative.
