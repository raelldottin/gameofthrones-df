# Malachite — Forest's Instinct

Source: Dragon Pit screenshot supplied June 24, 2026.

## Current State

- **Dragon:** Malachite
- **Habit:** Forest's Instinct
- **Habit level:** 1

## Effect

Each round, Malachite has a **35% chance** to affect the 2 other allies in any lane for 2 rounds:

- Increase **Physical Damage Dealt** by **+8%**, excluding Basic Attacks.
- Reduce **Tactical Damage Received** by **-8%**.

Because the effect specifies the 2 other allies, Malachite does not buff itself.

## Tactical Interpretation

Forest's Instinct combines offense and protection:

- Physical command users gain additional damage, but Basic Attacks do not benefit.
- Both affected allies gain Tactical damage resistance.
- The two-round duration gives the effect useful uptime, though the screenshot does not establish whether repeated activations stack or only refresh the duration.

In the Malachite–Vhagar–Rhysarion formation, the Physical damage bonus directly supports Vhagar's Fiery Bonds, while both Vhagar and Rhysarion benefit from the Tactical damage reduction.
