# Hatchery — Sheepstealer & Vermax Featured Dragon Chest

Source: in-game Hatchery screenshots supplied June 24, 2026.

## Chest Summary

- **Chest:** Sheepstealer & Vermax Featured Dragon Chest
- **Cost:** 120 Gold per opening
- **10 openings:** 1,200 Gold
- **Rewards per opening:** 3
- **Featured dragons:** Sheepstealer (Legendary) and Vermax (Epic)
- **Possible special drops:** Elite Drop = 100 relics; Super Drop = 1,000 relics
- **Observed pity counters:** Vermax Elite 0/130; Sheepstealer Elite 0/250
- **Displayed time remaining:** 12 days, 8 hours

There is no displayed bulk discount: ten openings cost exactly ten times the single-opening price.

## Pity System

- A featured **Vermax Epic Elite Drop** is guaranteed every **130 pulls**.
- A featured **Sheepstealer Legendary Elite Drop** is guaranteed every **250 pulls**.
- The in-game description states that an Epic Elite Drop does not change the chance of receiving ordinary Epic relics, and a Legendary Elite Drop does not change the chance of receiving ordinary Legendary relics.

## Crimson Odds

Crimson appears only in Reward 2's Legendary pool.

| Crimson result | Chance per chest opening |
|---|---:|
| 5 relics | 0.60000% |
| 100 relics | 0.02375% |
| 1,000 relics | 0.00125% |
| **Any Crimson relic drop** | **0.62500%** |

Calculations:

- 5 relics: `10% Legendary × 6.25% Crimson × 96% regular = 0.60000%`
- 100 relics: `10% × 6.25% × 3.8% = 0.02375%`
- 1,000 relics: `10% × 6.25% × 0.2% = 0.00125%`

This is approximately one Crimson-bearing opening per 160 openings before considering variance.

### Seven-opening example

At the observed balance of **894 Gold**, the player can purchase seven single openings for 840 Gold, leaving 54 Gold.

- Chance of at least one Crimson drop in seven openings: **4.2938%**
- Chance of no Crimson drop in seven openings: **95.7062%**
- Expected Crimson relics across seven openings: **0.46375 relics**

This chest is therefore inefficient when the sole objective is upgrading Crimson.

## Roster-Development Interpretation

This is a broad roster-development chest rather than a targeted Crimson chest.

It can support several current five-army dragons:

- Rare: Dawnseeker, Jagadrix, Antares, Arrax, Nyrena, Shimmer, Vesper, Arulix, Solstryker and Bevlorin
- Epic: Rhysarion, Tashix, Velar, Feskar and others
- Legendary: Crimson, Vhagar, Malachite, Syrax and Seasmoke

For a specific Rare dragon, the chance of receiving at least one relic-bearing result in a single opening across all three reward slots is approximately **19.0721%**. Across seven openings, that rises to approximately **77.2654%**.

## Spending Guidance

- Do not spend Gold here solely to target Crimson.
- Free Dragonglass Keys are reasonable to use because the chest develops many roster dragons.
- Pursuing Vermax or Sheepstealer is more coherent when the player intends to make meaningful progress toward the 130- or 250-pull pity counters.
- Single openings preserve the option to stop after a useful result and cost the same per pull as a ten-opening purchase.
