## Spending Decision

Do not spend Gold on this chest at the observed balance.

Reasons:

- Three pulls are much more likely to return no Syrax relics than any Syrax progress.
- The usual success is only 5 of the required 40 relics.
- The pity counters remain far from their guarantees.
- Spending 360 Gold leaves only 64 Gold and reduces flexibility for guaranteed market offers.

Use free Dragonglass Keys if acquired before the event ends. Save Gold for guaranteed Syrax relic offers or another purchase that completes the 2★ unlock.

## Strategic Context

Syrax's next star unlocks **Mindful Synergy**, which gives all three allies +6.5% Intelligence and Instinct for the entire battle at habit level 1. This makes Syrax relics a high priority, but it does not make low-volume random Gold pulls efficient.
