Arulix is primarily a control and counter-damage Champion. Instinct is the highest relevant offensive stat, supporting Gleaming Spiral’s Tactical Damage, while Strength supports the additional Physical Damage unlocked at 6★.

## Active Command — Gleaming Spiral

Each round:

- 25% chance to reduce Fire Damage Dealt by **15%** for 1 round for every enemy that deals Fire Damage.

On rounds 1, 2, 3, 5, and 8:

- deal Tactical Damage to all enemies that deal non-Basic-Attack Physical Damage;
- displayed Damage Rate: **+45%**;
- Tactical Damage scales with Arulix’s Instinct and is mitigated by the target’s Intelligence.

At 6★ or higher, through Spiral Surge, on those same rounds:

- deal Physical Damage to two enemies in any lane;
- base Damage Rate: **+20%**;
- round 5 Damage Rate: **+30%**;
- round 8 Damage Rate: **+40%**;
- Physical Damage scales with Arulix’s Strength and is mitigated by the target’s Instinct.

## Current Habit — Hypnotic Helix

Hypnotic Helix is unlocked and currently at level 1.

On rounds 1, 3, 6, and 8:

- chance to **Overwhelm** one enemy in any lane for 2 rounds;
- prioritizes Fire Damage dealers;
- Overwhelm prevents Active Commands and Habits on the target’s turn.

On rounds 2, 4, 7, and 9:

- chance to **Stagger** one enemy in any lane for 2 rounds;
- prioritizes Physical Damage dealers;
- Stagger prevents Attack Modifier Commands and Basic Attacks on the target’s turn.

The observed level-two upgrade requires:

- 100 blue Rare cores;
- 100 green Champion Habit cores;
- the account currently has 24 and 80 respectively;
- the interface displays a 40-Gold bypass because both materials are short.

Do not use Gold for this upgrade. The improvement from 12.5% to 15% is too small for the premium-currency cost.

## Development Breakpoints

- **Current 3★:** repeated Overwhelm and Stagger checks plus Fire-damage suppression and anti-Physical Tactical attacks.
- **4★:** Battle Cunning reduces both Strength and Intelligence for all enemies.
- **6★:** Spiral Surge adds repeated two-target Physical Damage.
- **8★:** Iron Shell gives both partners permanent protection against Physical-command and Fire Damage.
- **10★:** Mimicry copies important negative effects to enemies and positive effects to allies.

## Current Investment Decision

Arulix is worth continuing to develop, but the next meaningful gain is **4★ Battle Cunning**, not a Gold-funded Hypnotic Helix upgrade. Prioritize guaranteed Arulix relics and save Gold.
