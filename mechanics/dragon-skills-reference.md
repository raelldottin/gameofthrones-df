## Active Skills (Combat Abilities)

### Arulix (Champion Whelp) — Gleaming Spiral
- **Every round:** 25% chance to reduce Fire Damage dealt by *all enemies that deal Fire damage* by 15% for 1 round
- **On rounds 1, 2, 3, 5, 8:** Deals **Tactical Damage** to *all enemies that deal Physical Damage* (does not affect Basic-Attack-only enemies), with **+45% Damage Rate**
- **At 6+ Stars:** On rounds 1, 2, 3, 5, 8: Deals **Physical Damage** to 2 enemies in any combat lane, **+20% Damage Rate**. Boosted 1.5x (+30%) on round 5, 2x (+40%) on round 8

---

### Nyrena (Champion Hatchling) — Undermine
- **Tactical Damage** to target at **80% damage rate** (scales with attacker's Instinct, reduced by target's Intelligence)
- **Physical Damage Dealt Modifier:** Applies debuff reducing target's **Physical Damage received by 10%**

---

### Shimmer (Sentinel Whelp) — Unbreakable Loyalty
- **Tactical Damage** to target at **+50% damage rate** (scales with attacker's Instinct, reduced by target's Intelligence)
- **Strength Modifier:** Debuff lowering target's **Strength by 18%** (boosted by attacker's Instinct)
- **Initiative Modifier:** Debuff lowering target's **Initiative** (details partially cut off, boosted by Instinct)

---

### Arrax (Warrior Hatchling) — Sudden Strike
- **On rounds 2, 4, 6, 8:** 25% chance to apply **Weakened** debuff (reduces target's damage dealt) to 1 enemy adjacent to primary target. **Chance doubles to 50% if target already has Bleed**
- **On rounds 2, 4, 5, 6, 8:** Deals **Physical Damage** to 2 enemies adjacent to primary target, at **+40% damage rate**
