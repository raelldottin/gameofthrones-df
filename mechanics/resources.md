# Resources

## Basic Resources (Expire at Reign End)
- food:
    icon: wheat
    role: troops and population
- wood:
    icon: log
    role: buildings and upgrades
- stone:
    icon: rock
    role: buildings and upgrades
- iron:
    icon: bar
    role: troops, weapons, siege
- meat:
    icon: drumstick
    role: dragon feeding only
- stamina:
    icon: lightning
    role: movement and actions
- resolve:
    icon: flame
    role: hero abilities or morale
- territory_influence:
    icon: hexagon
    role: map control
