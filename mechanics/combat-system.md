# Combat System

## Army Structure
- max_armies: 5
- dragons_per_army: 3
- vanguard_required: true
- total_dragons_used: 15
- total_dragons_owned: 16

## Battle Rules
- max_rounds: 10
- stalemate_enabled: true
- stalemate_rule: both armies stay on tile after 10 rounds
- lane_mechanic: winning lane merges into adjacent for 2v1

## Two-Layer Siege
- phase_1_field_battle: tile defenders
- phase_2_siege: durability
- siege_requirement: Siege Troops for Points of Interest tiles

## Challenge Rating
- excludes: troop type and dragon affinity
- tiers: Impossible, Challenging, Even, Favorable, Easy
