## Two Parallel Upgrade Tracks

| Track | What It Upgrades | Resources | Max Level |
|-------|------------------|-----------|-----------|
| **Dragon Stars** | Base stats, rarity, unlocks enhanced skill effects | Hatchery Chest (Keys/Gold) | 5★ (or more) |
| **Skill Levels** | Individual skill potency (damage, duration, power) | Cores + Breedmarks + Gold | 5 per skill |

---

## Dragon Stars (Hatchery System)

| Requirement | Source |
|-------------|--------|
| **Hatchery Chest Keys** | Wild Dragon hunts, events, daily rewards |
| **Gold** | General currency, market purchases |

**Note:** Each star increases base stats and may unlock enhanced skill effects (e.g., "At 6+ Stars" effects seen in Dragon Pit skills like Arulix's Gleaming Spiral).

## Resource Farming Strategy

### For Star Upgrades (Hatchery Keys)
1. **Daily Wild Dragon hunts** → Keys drop
2. **Event chests** (Dragon Egg timer 10h 45m seen in IMG_2845)
3. **Gold Market** if keys available for purchase

### For Skill Upgrades (Cores + Breedmarks)
1. **Match dragon class to Wild Dragon type:**
   - Need **Hunter Breedmarks** (Tashix, Nyrena)? → Hunt **Wild Hunter** (weak to Champion) with Champion dragons
   - Need **Sentinel Breedmarks** (Shimmer, Vesper)? → Hunt **Wild Sentinel** (weak to Hunter) with Hunter dragons
   - Need **Warrior Breedmarks** (Jagadrix, Vhagar)? → Hunt **Wild Warrior** (weak to Sentinel) with Sentinel dragons
   - Need **Champion Cores** (Velar, Arrax, Solstryker)? → Hunt **Wild Champion** (weak to Warrior) with Warrior dragons

2. **Gold/Shards Market** for direct purchase when farming insufficient
