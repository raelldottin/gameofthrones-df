## Unit Upgrades (War Tactics)
- **Tier I maxes**: Spearmen Damage I is maxed at Level 5; Shieldbearers Damage I visible at lower levels
- **Tier II damage upgrades**: Spearmen/Shieldbearers/Cavalry/Archers Damage II require prior Tier I maxed, are expensive, but provide +1.20% damage and +490 Power
- **Current blocked candidates (resource starved)**:
  - Siege Precision 6→7 (needs 40k Wood/Stone/Silver)
  - Combat Training 4→5 (needs 55k each; Wood deficit noted)
  - Cavalry Damage I 3→4 (needs 65k each; Wood/Stone deficit)

- Costs resources and time; benefit is faster dragon leveling during PvE
