## Active Command — Fiery Bonds

Each round:

- **25% chance** to Taunt all three enemies in any lane for 2 rounds;
- the chance doubles to **50%** against enemies afflicted with Burn.

Taunt forces the target to direct its Basic Attack at Vhagar.

On every even-numbered round:

- deal Physical Damage to one enemy within adjacency;
- displayed Damage Rate: **+120%**;
- Physical Damage scales with Vhagar's Strength and is mitigated by the target's Instinct.

Fiery Bonds combines recurring control with reliable Physical-command pressure. Its Burn interaction becomes more valuable when another army member can apply Burn consistently.
