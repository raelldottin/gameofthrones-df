## Skill 1: Breath of Fire (Command — Active)

**Rounds 2, 5, 8:** Deal Fire Damage to 2 Enemies within adjacency (Damage Rate: +100%). Damage increased by 1.5x if target afflicted with Panic (Damage Rate: +150%).

**At 10 Stars:**
- Rounds 2, 5, 8: Deal Fire Damage to 1 Enemy in any lane (DR: +60%) + 40% chance to inflict Burn for 2 rounds
- Then deal Fire Damage to a different Enemy in any lane (DR: +30%) + 20% chance to inflict Burn for 2 rounds

### Details
| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Fire Damage | DR: +100%, scales with Intelligence, mitigated by target's Initiative |
| Fire Damage (10★) | DR: +60%, same scaling |
| Burn (10★) | Fire DoT each round for 2 rounds (DR: +20%) |

## Key Takeaways

1. **Shadowsong is a strong debuffer** — Ensnare hits -18% Instinct/Initiative on Round 1, lasts 3 rounds, hits 2 targets. At L5 = -36%.
2. **Breath of Fire synergizes with Panic** — 1.5x multiplier. Need a Panic source in same army.
3. **Intelligence-scaling** — Both skills enhanced by Intelligence (Shadowsong's highest stat at 115.2). Good fit for Hunter class.
4. **Daemoros upgraded to 3★** — significant jump from previously noted 1★.
5. **Venator** — new dragon name not previously in roster.
