# Feskar (Champion Hatchling, Lv 34, 3★) — Calculated Assault (Command)

### Calculated Assault (Active)
- **Physical Damage Dealt Modifier:** Reduce target's Physical Damage Dealt by **-12%**
- **Fire Damage (At 6+ Stars):** Deal **Fire Damage** to target (Damage Rate: **+40%**)
  - Scales with attacker's **Intelligence**, mitigated by target's **Initiative**
  - Unlocks at 6★ rarity
