## 6★ — Cunning Ruse

Locked in the observed 2★ state; the screen requires 4 more Tashix stars.

Each round:

- 25% chance to gain 1 Mirage stack, up to 10;
- each Mirage stack increases Tashix's Fire Damage Dealt by 2.5% until the end of combat;
- the maximum Fire Damage bonus from 10 stacks is +25%.

While Tashix has at least 4 Mirage stacks, each round separately checks all three enemies for **Weakened (-15% Damage Dealt)** for 2 rounds. The activation chance is doubled against enemies that deal Tactical Damage.

| Habit level | Base Weakened chance | Versus Tactical enemy | Power |

| 1 | 10% | 20% | 340 |
| 2 | 12% | 24% | 740 |
| 3 | 14% | 28% | 1,200 |
| 4 | 17% | 34% | 1,800 |
| 5 | 20% | 40% | 2,500 |

Cunning Ruse confirms that Tashix is fundamentally a Fire-damage attacker with anti-Tactical control.

## Development Breakpoints

- **Current 2★:** Enervate supplies reliable anti-Tactical mitigation but no direct Fire scaling.
- **4★:** Dragon's Cunning adds permanent Intelligence and enemy Instinct manipulation.
- **6★:** Cunning Ruse is the main identity breakpoint, adding stack-based Fire scaling and possible team-wide Weakened.
- **8★:** Battle Guile adds a permanent three-enemy Instinct and Initiative reduction.
- **10★:** Veiled Ambush adds a large once-per-combat Fire strike after enough Mirage stacks accumulate.

## Upgrade Priority

Do not spend 170 Gold to raise Enervate from level 1 to level 2. The upgrade is matchup-specific and the account lacks both required core types.

Tashix's more valuable progression comes from additional stars:

1. **Reach 4★** for Dragon's Cunning.
2. **Reach 6★** for Cunning Ruse.
3. **Reach 8★** for Battle Guile.
4. Upgrade Enervate later with naturally earned cores when Tactical-damage opponents are a recurring problem.

## Syrax Archer-Army Implications

Current Archer formation:

- **Left:** Tashix
- **Vanguard:** Syrax
- **Right:** Antares

The new Habit data confirms that Tashix is Fire-oriented, not a Tactical-damage specialist. This matters when Syrax eventually unlocks Tactical Inferno at 8★:

- Syrax prioritizes a Tactical-damage increase for the left flank.
- Syrax prioritizes a Fire-damage increase for the right flank.
- Antares currently occupies the correct right-flank Fire slot.
- Tashix on the left would not fully exploit the Tactical-damage priority.

Keep the current formation while Syrax remains below 8★. Before Tactical Inferno unlocks, compare Tashix with a true Tactical-damage Archer-affinity candidate for the left flank. Tashix can remain a strong Fire attacker, but only one Fire-oriented flank receives Syrax's prioritized Fire buff.
