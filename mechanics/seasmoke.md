## Active Command — Cleansing Wrath

Each round:

- perform up to three separate checks to remove one positive effect from an enemy;
- each check has a **20% chance** and selects one enemy in any lane.

On rounds 3, 6, and 9:

- deal Fire Damage to one enemy in the same lane;
- displayed Damage Rate: **+190%**;
- Fire Damage scales with Seasmoke's Intelligence and is mitigated by the target's Initiative.

At 6★ or higher, the command also gains the Infectious Wrath effects documented below.

## 6★ — Infectious Wrath

Locked in the observed 1★ state; the screen requires five more Seasmoke stars.

Cleansing Wrath gains:

- whenever Seasmoke Cleanses an enemy, apply one stack of Infectious Wrath for 3 rounds;
- maximum 3 stacks;
- each stack reduces Recovery Received;
- on rounds 3, 6, and 9, deal Physical Damage to two adjacent enemies;
- this Physical Damage is doubled against targets afflicted with Panic.

## Development Breakpoints

- **Current 1★:** Cleansing Wrath provides buff removal and a high-rate Fire strike on rounds 3, 6, and 9.
- **2★:** Clever Maneuver adds a large permanent Intelligence and Initiative buff to the highest-Intelligence ally.
- **4★:** Wind's Favor improves the entire army's Initiative.
- **6★:** Infectious Wrath adds anti-healing and Physical damage, with extra value against Panic targets.
- **8★:** Cunning Ferocity turns Seasmoke into a two-flank Fire and Intelligence amplifier.
- **10★:** Loyal Bond adds adaptive offense or defense based on allied troop capacity.

## Current Army Implications

Current Cavalry formation:

- **Left:** Arulix
- **Vanguard:** Seasmoke
- **Right:** Shimmer

The screenshots correct Seasmoke's class to **Champion**, not Sentinel, and correct the active command to **Cleansing Wrath**. Loyal Bond is the future 10★ Habit rather than the current command.

The current vanguard placement remains reasonable because:

- Seasmoke has the highest displayed power in the formation;
- Cleansing Wrath can remove enemy positive effects from any lane;
- Cunning Ferocity at 8★ requires adjacency to affect both flanks.

At 2★, identify which member has the highest in-battle Intelligence because Clever Maneuver automatically targets that ally. At 8★, reevaluate whether Arulix and Shimmer provide enough Fire-damage value to justify keeping both beside Seasmoke, since Cunning Ferocity specifically amplifies Fire Damage Dealt.
