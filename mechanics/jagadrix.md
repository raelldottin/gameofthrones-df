## Upgrade Priority

### Current unlocked habits

1. **Whispering Sabotage** — best general upgrade. Raising the activation chance improves repeated same-lane damage suppression against every damage type.
2. **Enervate** — strong but matchup-dependent because it only affects an enemy that deals Tactical Damage.
3. **Second Wind** — useful in long fights, but it activates only on round 6 and level 1 already supplies a large Recovery effect.

### Future habits

1. **Echoes of Deceit** — highest long-term priority. It combines enemy Instinct and Initiative reduction with repeated anti-Tactical Fire attacks.
2. **Quick-Witted** — strengthens Jagadrix's Intelligence and Initiative and therefore supports Echoes of Deceit's scaling.

## Current Investment Decision

The observed inventory showed 119 blue Rare cores, 110 green leaf-emblem Habit cores, and 725 Gold. One current Habit upgrade would consume 100 of each core type.

Do not use Gold to cover a shortage. If green Habit cores are still being reserved for Malachite's Forest's Instinct, hold Jagadrix's habits at level 1. When resources are available specifically for Jagadrix, upgrade Whispering Sabotage first.

## Crimson Army Role

Jagadrix currently remains the left flank of the Spearmen formation:

- **Left:** Jagadrix
- **Vanguard:** Dawnseeker
- **Right:** Crimson

Whispering Sabotage improves this formation's early and mid-fight survival. Echoes of Deceit is the future breakpoint that most directly supports Crimson because reducing enemy Initiative improves Fire damage effectiveness.
