- Dragon Growth is not only the Dragon Troop Capacity linear tree
- There is also an **Amplifier I** node buffing Heirloom effectiveness
- **Class-specific nodes** exist for Warrior/Champion/Hunter/Sentinel
- Nodes affect **Damage vs RSS Defenders** and **Incoming Damage vs RSS Defenders**

## Current Upgrade Blockers
- Wood deficit is recurring: 1,610 available vs 1,800 required

- Dragon Scales and Scrolls are abundant; Wood is the bottleneck

## Implication
- Dragon nodes are a parallel progression track to troop Dmg/Def research
- Wood is needed for both Stronghold/damage research and Dragon nodes
