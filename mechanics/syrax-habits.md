# Syrax — Habit Unlocks

Source: Dragon Pit screenshots supplied June 24, 2026.

## Current State

- **Dragon:** Syrax
- **Level:** 36
- **Stars:** 1★
- **Class:** Sentinel Hatchling
- **Command:** Blazing Fury
- **Command progress:** 0 / 40
- **XP:** 5,394 / 242,069
- **Positive troop affinities:** Spearmen and Archers
- **Negative troop affinity:** Siege

All five documented habits are currently locked by Syrax's star level.

## 2★ — Mindful Synergy

Unlock requirement shown: **earn 1 more Syrax star**.

### Effect

At the start of combat, increase the **Intelligence** and **Instinct** of all 3 allies in any lane until the end of combat. The effect is enhanced by Syrax's **Initiative**.

| Habit level | Intelligence and Instinct | Power |
|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 | +6.5% | 420 |
| 2 | +7.8% | 920 |
| 3 | +9.1% | 1,500 |
| 4 | +11.05% | 2,200 |
| 5 | +13% | 3,100 |

This is Syrax's most important near-term breakpoint because it improves the entire army for the full battle.

- Both effects are enhanced by Syrax's **Instinct**.

## 6★ — Strategic Revival

Unlock requirement shown: **earn 5 more Syrax stars**.

### Effect added to Blazing Fury

On rounds **2, 5, and 8**:

- Apply Recovery to the ally with the least troops.
- Base level-1 Recovery Rate: **+50%**, enhanced by Syrax's Initiative.
- If an enemy is afflicted with **Slow**, Recovery is increased by 1.5× to **+75%** at level 1.
- There is a chance to grant the target **Resistance**, reducing Damage Received by **20%** for 2 rounds.

| Habit level | Resistance chance | Recovery Rate |
|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 | 40% | 50% |
| 2 | 52% | 60% |
| 3 | 64% | 70% |
| 4 | 80% | 85% |
| 5 | 100% | 100% |

The 1.5× Slow bonus implies Recovery Rates of 75%, 90%, 105%, 127.5%, and 150% before any in-game cap or other modifier; the screenshots do not establish whether Recovery is capped.

## 8★ — Tactical Inferno

Unlock requirement shown: **earn 7 more Syrax stars**.

### Effect

At the start of round 1 for 3 rounds:

- Increase the **Tactical Damage Dealt** of 1 ally, prioritizing the **left flank**.
- Increase the **Fire Damage Dealt** of 1 ally, prioritizing the **right flank**.

| Habit level | Tactical and Fire Damage | Power |
|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 | +18% | 420 |
| 2 | +21.6% | 920 |
| 3 | +25.2% | 1,500 |
| 4 | +30.6% | 2,200 |
| 5 | +36% | 3,100 |

This validates keeping a Tactical-oriented ally on Syrax's left and a Fire-oriented ally on Syrax's right. In the current Archer formation, Antares is correctly positioned on the right.

## 10★ — Mother's Mercy

Unlock requirement shown: **earn 9 more Syrax stars**.

### Effect

Each round, Syrax has a chance to cleanse from 1 ally in any lane:

- 2 Negative effects; and
- 1 Control effect.

The cleanse prioritizes an ally afflicted with a Control effect. Control effects listed in the screenshot include **Stun, Stagger, Overwhelm, and Confusion**.

| Habit level | Cleanse chance | Power |
|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 | 14% | 430 |
| 2 | 18.2% | 1,000 |
| 3 | 22.4% | 1,700 |
| 4 | 28% | 2,700 |
| 5 | 35% | 4,000 |

## Army Implications

Current formation:

- **Left:** Tashix
- **Vanguard:** Syrax
- **Right:** Antares
- **Troops:** Archers

Syrax remains the correct vanguard because it is the Sentinel and its future habits are team-wide support effects. Antares should remain on the right to receive Tactical Inferno's Fire-damage priority. Tashix remains on the left provisionally; its full current skill data should be reviewed before Tactical Inferno unlocks to confirm that it is the best Tactical-damage recipient.

## Investment Priority

The next Syrax star is a high-value breakpoint because Mindful Synergy affects all three allies for the entire combat. Guaranteed Syrax relic offers should therefore be prioritized over random chest pulls and over slow Legendary-Core accumulation when the relic offer is reasonably priced.
